// design constraints

Built around you.
Not your tools.

Five hard requirements. No compromises.

01 ·

SMS First

The most universal interface on earth. No app, no account, no setup. Text a number. Your EA answers.

02 ·

Zero User Friction

Technical complexity lives with the operator — not the end user. Non-technical people just get a number to text.

03 ·

Extensible Core

Bring Your Own Agents. Full MCP support. Custom integrations. The core is minimal. The surface area is infinite.

04 ·

Secure by Design

Per-user credential isolation. Namespace separation at the storage layer. Rate limiting baked in from day one.

05 ·

User-Centered

Proactive + reactive. Work and personal. Stays relevant across job changes, life changes. A trusted memory of you.

// deployment model

How it works

For operators. For users. Different roles. One system.

STEP 01

Operator deploys

A technical person self-hosts The Operator. One deployment serves an entire circle. Infrastructure, credentials, user isolation — all handled.

STEP 02

Users get a number

Non-technical users get a phone number to text. No setup, no downloads, no configuration. That's the entire onboarding.

STEP 03

EA goes to work

Proactive and reactive. Calendar, email, memory, custom agents. Persistent context. Immediately useful, gets better over time.
